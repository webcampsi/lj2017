Venue and other info

This year, WebCamp will take place on April 22, 2017 at the Faculty of Computer and Information Science in Ljubljana. You can expect a day full of diverse talks, chocolate, coffee, and fun – it’s a Saturday and you can bring your kids too!

Detailed instructions on getting to the faculty. If you're arriving by car, you can use the parking space before the main gates. Green karma points if you carpool or take public transport. Cycling is another great option, as you can park your bike right in front of the faculty.

WebCamp is an event organized by the community for the community. All talks are in English and we expect 300+ attendees from all over the region. It’s free to attend, but you’ll need a ticket, so make sure to subscribe to our mailing list and be notified when tickets are released.

If you are currently a student of Faculty of Computer and Information Science, you can get a ticket by emailing us at info@webcamp.si.

Learn new things, meet new friends or get a massage! In the meantime, your kids can play together with Minecraft under responsible adult supervision. There will also be some cool VR and IoT experiences for all of us to play with.
