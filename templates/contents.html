Venue and other info

WebCamp will take place on Saturday, April 22, 2017 at the Faculty of Computer and Information Science in Ljubljana. You can expect a day full of diverse talks, workshops, chocolate, coffee, and fun gadgets.

WebCamp is an event organized by the community for the community. All talks are in English and we expect 500+ attendees from all over the region. It’s free to attend, but you need a ticket. This year's tickets are all sold out.

Detailed instructions on getting to the faculty.

If you're arriving by car, you can use the limited parking space before the main gates. To avoid dealing with parking and get back home safely, HOPIN taxi is just a tap away.

Green karma points if you carpool or take public transport. Cycling is another great option, as you can park your bike right in front of the faculty.

Check out some highlights from the previous year below — but keep in mind that this year’s WebCamp is going to be even more special!

Also see how much fun we had in 2015 & 2016
